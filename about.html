Hi, I am Daria Romanova

Front-end and Android developer based in Dnipro, Ukraine

My journey to the world of coding is as unique as my diverse background. In the past, I pursued a career in medicine with a heartfelt desire to make a difference in people's lives. Despite dedicating many years to my medical education and embarking on a promising scientific career, my parallel interest in programming led me to advocate for new technologies within a state university. I was a professor's assistent, I taught srudents and I really wanted to make educational process more interesting and modern.

The onset of war in Ukraine marked a turning point in my life, compelling my family to leave our home and prompting a career shift towards a profession that could be pursued anywhere in the world. This led me to choose frontend and mobile app development. The years spent in medical school honed my skills—teaching me perseverance, the ability to absorb vast amounts of information, and the determination never to give up. These qualities, coupled with my lifelong passion for music and ballet, contribute to my unique perspective.

I firmly believe that my qualities—determination, hard work, responsibility, and unwavering dedication—will propel me to achieve outstanding results in any endeavor and profession.

Thank you for letting me share a glimpse of my journey with you.
